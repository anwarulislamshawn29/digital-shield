What's New in Digital Shield 2.3

We've added some powerful new tools to enhance your privacy and browsing experience.

DNS Record Checker

Look under the hood of any website. You can now instantly check the DNS records (A, AAAA, MX, etc.) for the current site to see its server infrastructure and better understand where your data is going.

Secure Password Health Check

Check if your passwords have been exposed in a data breach directly from the popup. Using k-Anonymity, your full password is never sent to any server, ensuring your security is never compromised.

Digital Shadow

Visualize your online footprint. Get a clear, interactive map of the websites you visit and the trackers they connect to, helping you understand the breadth and depth of your digital shadow.
